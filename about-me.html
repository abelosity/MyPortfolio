Hey, I'm Abel.

I'm a product designer studying interaction and material design at the SFU School of Interactive Arts. I approach problems by utilizing ergonomic interaction design, research-driven ideation, exploring product interfaces, and thorough prototyping. I believe that a well-designed product can improve the user's everyday life, and that is the cusp of lasting impact. Outside of my design ventures, I am an origami enthusiast, doodler, TED talk fanatic and a fun-loving conversationalist.

I aspire to work in the realm of industrial design, working on beautiful, conscientious products that enhance the everyday experience with a minimal, clean aesthetic. I aim to explore the complexities behind the ergonomics of domestic use products, and ultimately begin a firm.

Contact me at abela@sfu.ca!
